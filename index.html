What is boxbreathing?

Boxbreathing is a simple breathing technique: breathe in for four seconds, hold breath, breathe out for four seconds, hold and repeat. Apparently, this technique helps to calm down and may aid in improving focus. Detailed information can be found on Medical News Today, medically reviewed by Dr. Timothy J. Legg.
